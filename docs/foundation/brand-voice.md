# THE DEEP MIRROR BRAND VOICE

## 1. VOICE SUMMARY
We speak like a perceptive colleague who sees patterns others miss. Confident but not arrogant, direct without being cold. We respect our audience's intelligence and time while acknowledging that genuine self-knowledge requires real work, not quick fixes.

## 2. TONE ATTRIBUTES

**Insightful:** We surface connections and patterns that aren't immediately obvious. We don't just state what something does; we explain why it matters in ways that make people think "I hadn't considered that."

**Pragmatic:** No mystical language or personality test fluff. We treat the Enneagram as a practical framework for understanding behavior, not a spiritual journey or identity badge.

**Respectful skepticism:** We acknowledge that existing tools fall short without dismissing the genuine insights people have found in them. We're critical of methods, never of the people using them.

**Conversational precision:** We choose words carefully but don't sound academic. Complex ideas delivered in plain language, like explaining something important to a smart friend.

**Quietly confident:** We know what we've built works better. We don't need to oversell it or attack competitors. The difference speaks for itself.

## 3. EXAMPLE SENTENCES

**Headline:** "Your Enneagram result gets more accurate every time you use it"

**CTA:** "Start your assessment"

**Paragraph opening:** "Static surveys assume you can summarize your deepest patterns in a single sitting."

**Technical explanation:** "The AI interviewer adapts its questions based on your previous responses, following threads that reveal inconsistencies or areas worth exploring further."

**Error message:** "Something went wrong on our end. Your conversation data is safe, and you can pick up where you left off once we're back online."

## 4. COUNTER-EXAMPLES

❌ "Unlock the secrets of your true self with revolutionary AI technology!"
❌ "Transform your life with the world's most advanced Enneagram assessment."
❌ "Join thousands of executives who have discovered their authentic leadership style."
❌ "Cutting-edge conversational AI meets ancient wisdom to optimize your personal growth."
❌ "The game-changing tool that will revolutionize how you understand yourself."

## 5. SELF-CHECK

✅ **Headline** creates specific curiosity about ongoing refinement vs. one-time results
✅ **CTA** is direct and low-friction without hype
✅ **Paragraph opening** sets up a problem through contrast, not complaint
✅ **Technical explanation** describes capability without jargon or overselling
✅ **Error message** maintains trust while being practical about technical reality

Each example demonstrates the voice attributes while serving its specific context. The counter-examples show typical SaaS/self-help language we explicitly avoid.
