# Design Principles for The Deep Mirror

## Conversational Depth
Typography creates a sense of intimate dialogue. Work Sans for headings has the geometric clarity to build trust in AI-powered assessment while maintaining approachability — it's serious without being clinical. Crimson Text for body copy evokes the considered pace of meaningful conversation. Unlike the rushed feeling of most digital type, Crimson Text invites reading, reflection, and depth. JetBrains Mono for any technical elements preserves the sense that something sophisticated is happening under the hood.

## Psychological Safety
Colors establish an environment where difficult self-examination feels safe rather than threatening. Forest green primary `#047857` commands attention without aggression — it's grounded, stable, trustworthy. The warm cream backgrounds `#FEFDF8` and `#F9F7F1` create an intimate, private space that white cannot achieve. Orange accent `#EA580C` provides warmth and energy without overstimulation. This isn't a sterile assessment environment — it's a thoughtful space for authentic self-discovery.

## Generous Space for Reflection
Dense layouts rush people toward answers. Generous spacing acknowledges that real self-knowledge takes time. Section gaps of 56-72px allow ideas to breathe. Card padding of 32-40px creates containment without compression. Line height of 1.7 for body text slows reading to the pace of contemplation rather than consumption.

## Sophisticated Restraint
The visual hierarchy guides attention without manipulation. Each element earns its placement through purpose, not decoration. Form elements look substantial enough to warrant thoughtful responses. Trust signals appear naturally in the flow rather than as desperate badges. The overall impression should be that of a well-designed private practice — confident, established, and focused entirely on the person in front of them.
