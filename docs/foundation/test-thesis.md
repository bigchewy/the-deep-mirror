# THE DEEP MIRROR STRATEGY

## 1. WHO IS OUR SMALLEST VIABLE AUDIENCE?

Anyone who wants to understand themselves better and is willing to have a real conversation to get there.

They may have heard about the Enneagram from a podcast, a friend, or a workplace retreat. Or they may never have heard of it at all. What they share is a genuine desire for self-knowledge, not a certification or a coaching budget.

This is a broad audience. That is intentional. The Enneagram is already popular. Millions of people take personality assessments every year. We are not fishing in a small pond. We are offering a better net.

The person we are most useful to is curious, a little skeptical of simple answers, and willing to spend more than five minutes on themselves. They do not need to be an executive. They do not need to have been burned by a bad test before. They just need to be ready for a real question instead of a multiple choice.

## 2. WHAT MAKES US REMARKABLE TO THEM?

We are the first Enneagram tool that grows with you.

Every other assessment gives you a type and sends you away. One session. One result. Static.

We work differently in two ways.

First, the conversation itself is dynamic. We do not ask you to pick A, B, or C. We ask you a question, listen to your answer, and ask a better question. When your answers conflict, we notice. When you seem to be describing who you want to be rather than who you are, we go deeper. A static survey cannot do this. We can.

Second, we do not force a single type on your first visit. You might finish your first session and hear: "You're showing strong tendencies toward Type 8, with meaningful patterns that also suggest 6 and 2. Come back in two weeks. Between now and then, watch for these specific things in yourself. Your next session will be different because of what you've already told us."

That is not a failure to type you. That is honesty about how personality actually works. The number is not the point. The self-knowledge is the point. We earn it together, over time.

No other tool does this. Not because they haven't thought of it. Because a survey cannot.

## 3. WHAT'S OUR PERMISSION TO REACH THEM?

We show up wherever people are already searching for self-understanding.

"What Enneagram type am I"
"Enneagram test free"
"Why do I get different Enneagram types on different tests"
"Best personality test for self-improvement"
"Enneagram vs Myers-Briggs"

We earn permission by being genuinely useful before asking for anything. Content that explains what the Enneagram actually measures. Content that names why static tests fall short. Content that invites people into a different kind of conversation.

We do not interrupt. We attract.
